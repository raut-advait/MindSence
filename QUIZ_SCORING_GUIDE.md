# Mental Health Quiz - Score Guide

## ✅ What Score Should I Get?

The quiz evaluates your mental health on a scale of **0-40**, where:
- **0-5**: Excellent Mental Well-being (minimal risk)
- **6-15**: Good Mental Health (some stress, manageable)
- **16-29**: Moderate Stress Detected (needs attention)
- **30-39**: High Stress & Anxiety (seek support)
- **40**: Severe Distress (immediate support needed)

---

## 📊 Quick Reference

| If you answer mostly... | Your score will be... | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 1s and 5s | **1-5** | ✅ Excellent health |
| 1s, 2s, with 4s/5s | **3-12** | ✅ Good health |
| Mix of 2s and 3s | **10-19** | ⚠️ Some stress |
| Mix of 3s and 4s | **15-30** | ⚠️ Moderate to high stress |
| 4s and 5s | **25-40** | 🚨 Severe stress |

## ❓ Why Different Scales?

Some questions use **opposite scales**:

**Risk factors (1 is GOOD):**
- Stress: 1=None, 5=Extreme
- Anxiety: 1=Never, 5=Constant
- Sadness: 1=Happy, 5=Very Sad
- Overwhelm: 1=Calm, 5=Extreme

**Protective factors (5 is GOOD):**
- Sleep Quality: 1=Poor, 5=Excellent
- Focus: 1=Can't concentrate, 5=Excellent
- Social Connection: 1=Isolated, 5=Very connected
- Energy: 1=Exhausted, 5=Very High

This is normal - just answer honestly about your current state!

---

## 💡 Tips for Accurate Assessment

1. **Answer honestly** - The quiz works best with truthful responses
2. **Think about the past week** - Answer based on recent patterns
3. **Don't overthink** - Your first response is usually most accurate  
4. **Remember the scale** - 1 is the worst for some, 5 is worst for others
5. **Take action** - Use your score to guide next steps (counseling, coping strategies, etc.)
