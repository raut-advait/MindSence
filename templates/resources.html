Campus Support Resources

Most colleges offer free counseling services to students. Check with your college:

  • 📚 Student Counseling Center
  • 🏥 Health Office or Medical Center
  • 👨‍💼 Dean of Students Office
  • 🤝 Peer Support Programs
  • 📋 Disability Services
💡 Pro Tip
Most campus counseling is FREE and confidential. Don't hesitate to reach out!
